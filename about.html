This is where you get to know me! Thanks again for visiting my site. Outside of my scientific interests, I mostly enjoy outdoors activities. Whether its hiking, camping, running or biking, I'm usually up for getting up and moving around in nature. Salt Lake City is a pretty perfect place for that, so I lucked out going to graduate school there. Aside from being active, I enjoy reading, cooking, traveling, weightlifting, spontaneous dancing, and pretty much any type of cooperative or competitive game. In my spare time I also like to photograph nature and people (see my picture above), and its a hobby I've developed over the past few years. Please feel free to contact me to order prints of any size.

I'm also a pretty big sports fan. Yay sports! I watch international rugby matches from time to time to reminisce about my time on the field at Vassar, since I was forced to retire due to injuries. As a New England native growing up, I am also a huge baseball and Boston Red Sox and Boston Celtics fan.
